Human Resources Management and Scheduling Project

Introduction

The aim of this project is to develop a comprehensive solution for efficient human resources management, focusing on resource allocation to services, staff scheduling, and planning of shifts, days off, and vacations. The solution will optimize staff allocation based on demand and historical data, ensuring an adequate response to organizational needs, especially during peak periods.

Key Features

  • Human Resources Management
    • Employee data registration and maintenance.
    • Performance monitoring and work history tracking.
  • Resource Allocation to Services
    • Assignment of staff to various tasks and services based on skills and availability.
    • Adjustment of assignments in response to operational needs.
  • Scheduling and Shift Planning
    • Automatic creation of weekly and monthly schedules.
    • Management of shifts and rosters, considering employee preferences and constraints.
  • Days Off and Vacation Management
    • Planning and approval of days off and vacations.
    • Ensuring adequate coverage during absences, minimizing operational impact.
  • Preparation for High-Demand Periods
    • Analysis of historical data to anticipate peak demand periods.
    • Proactive adjustments in staff allocation to ensure efficiency and service quality.

Objectives

  • Enhancing Operational Efficiency - Optimize human resources management, reduce operational costs, and improve service quality.
  • Increasing Employee Satisfaction - Ensure a fair balance between personal and professional life through transparent and efficient scheduling and leave management.
  • Proactive Response to Business Needs - Enable agile and effective responses to demand fluctuations, preventing staff overload and ensuring customer satisfaction.
