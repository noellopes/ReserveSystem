Group 8 - Room and Equipment Reservation

Project Responsibilities

Group 8 is tasked with creating and managing resources, including rooms and equipment, for event reservation purposes. Our responsibilities cover defining availability, setting schedules, and managing budgeting needs. Here’s a summary of our tasks:

  • Creation and management of various resources (rooms, equipment, etc.).
  • Setting availability, schedules, capacities, preparation time, and specific rules for each resource/room.
  • Defining types of events, such as parties, conferences, etc.
  • Budgeting requirements and calculations.
